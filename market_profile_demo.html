TPO Chart (left) • Volume Profile (right) • Side by Side • Dark Theme

POC

Point of Control

Price with most TPO letters and highest volume. Gold bar/line on both charts. Market's fairest price — strong magnet for return.

VALUE AREA

Value Area (VAH / VAL)

Contains 70% of all TPOs and 70% of volume. Purple highlight on both charts. Price outside VA = "out of value" → expect rejection or return.

IB

Initial Balance (A+B)

First 60 minutes. Green zone on TPO. Wide IB = market already moved, no extension likely. Narrow IB = explosive day ahead.

TAIL

Buying / Selling Tail

Single-letter price extremes. Red zone. Price was rejected instantly. Tip of tail = your stop loss. VP shows very thin bar here (low volume).

HVN / LVN

High / Low Volume Nodes

VP exclusive. HVN = wide bar = support/resistance. LVN = thin bar = price sliced through fast = same as single print in TPO.

TPO vs VP

Why Use Both?

TPO shows time at price. VP shows actual volume at price. Together they confirm: is this level important by time AND by money?
